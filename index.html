Yoga Mudras

Hand Gestures to Ground and Focus Your Practice

Mudras are hand gestures and sometimes physical movements which give a specific direction and shape to your yoga practice.

Two do the basic mudras are Jnana mudra(Knowledge Mudra) and Dhyana Mudra (Meditation Mudra).

Jnana Mudra

Jnana Mudra is performed by bringing the tips of the thumb and forefingers together while extending the remaining three fingers.

Dhyana Mudra

Dhyana Mudra is performed by turning the palms face up and stacking the hands with the right on top. The tips of the thumbs touch.
